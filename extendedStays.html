Extended stays

Our Studio is perfect for an extended stay in Abuja. It is the perfect place to relax and rejuvenate after a hard day of work. At 40 m2 the Studio offers all the facilities and amenities travellers could require.

The Studio

The Studio offers a king size bed, allergy-free and eco-friendly duvets and pillows, kitchenette, working desk, armchair, free high-speed Wi-Fi internet, large flat screen TV with news, sports and entertainment channels and many other details that provide an extra level of comfort that makes it perfect for the extended stay.
