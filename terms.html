Terms and Conditions

Nordic Villa is a well-established, small boutique hotel in Jai, Abuja just 10 minutes from the city center. Nordic Villa offers 15 suites and rooms furnished with modern Scandinavian design and located in a peaceful environment suited for both business and leisure travellers.

1. RESERVATION AND CONFIRMATION OF RESERVATION

A reservation made via e-mail, website, phone, or in-person to use Nordic Villa services is not confirmed until the full payment has been made. The room is at your disposal from 3 pm on the day of arrival until 12 noon on the day of departure, unless other arrangements have been made.

2. PAYMENT

Cash: We accept Naira, US Dollars, and Euros at the rates specified on our website. Please note that the full amount must be settled at the time of check-in. Bank transfer: Please contact us if you wish to make a bank transfer and we will provide you with our bank details. Please note that we require the full amount to appear on our account before your arrival. For foreign payments please note that this might take between 4 – 6 banking days

3. ROOM PRICES

Nordic Villa guarantees that all reservations made online will be at the best available price for the room of your choice on the date of booking. This is our commitment to providing our customers with maximum value for money. Room prices are per room, per night and are exclusive of VAT. A maximum of 2 adults and 2 children under the age of 12 years are allowed in each room. Breakfast, drinks, and snacks are not included in the room rate and must be paid for separately. Note that hotel prices in Abuja go up based on key events and during periods of high demand. For details of our most current room prices please check our website www.thenordicvilla.com or call us directly on 0809 994 4480 (+234 (0) 809 994 4480).

  • The Nordic Villa is on the right hand side no. 52
