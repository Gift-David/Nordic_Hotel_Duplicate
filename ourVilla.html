About Nordic Villa Abuja

Nordic Villa is a well-established, small boutique hotel in Jai, Abuja just 10 minutes from the city center. Nordic Villa offers 15 suites and rooms furnished with modern Scandinavian design and located in a peaceful environment suited for both business and leisure travellers.

THE Villa

The whole idea and philosophy behind Nordic Hotels Nigeria started back in 2011, right here at the Nordic Villa. A cozy little villa comprising 15 guest rooms and suites furnished in modern Scandinavian design. The peaceful and serene place is always ready to welcome you, whether it’s your first visit or of the many returning guests we pride ourselves on.

From the moment you call in to make a reservation we have just one thought on our mind and that’s to ensure your complete satisfaction. Whether this is your first visit to the capital city of Abuja or you are making another return, you will always be treated with the warmest welcome or as some would say as a return family member.

Each of our guest rooms and suites offer individually controlled air conditioning, complimentary WiFi, an ensuite bathroom, a work desk and a refreshing breakfast made to order that is served either at your room or at our restaurant.

Guests staying at the Nordic Villa also have access to the leisure facilities at the Nordic Hotel located just minutes away from each other. Facilities include a swimming pool, gym and a restaurant with an extensive international menu.
